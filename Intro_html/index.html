Universal Vacations Over The Years

Welcome to our travel blog!

Universal Studios is our happy place and we are excited to share our experiences with you.

We have been going to Universal Stuidos several times a year since 2021. Here we will document our vacations and share tips, tricks and things we have learned along the way to help you plan your next Universal vacation.

Our First Trip to Universal Studios

This was our first time visiting Universal Studios and we were amazed by the rides and attractions, our favorites seem to all be from the Harry Potter worlds:

  • Harry Potter and the Forbidden Journey
  • Hagrid’s Magical Creatures Motorbike Adventure
  • Harry Potter and the Escape from Gringotts
  • Harry Potter and the Battle at the Ministry
